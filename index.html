Who am I?

Ecclectic.

I am a budding software developer and woman in Tech who identifies as a feminist. I have been in tech for less than two years, I recently joined Waape as the CTO and founded the first Google Developer Students Club at my university, Makerere University Business School.
I am multi-faceted and organize sessions for debate, tech, Sexual Reproductive Health and anti-drugs campaigns. I've built...
I lead the DSC and double as the debate club president. In my spare time I engage my fellow students about SRH and do advocacy for safe sex pratices. I volunteer with Students Against Covid as a tech person, webinar host and digital help.
I have a passion for start-ups, learning new things and sharing knowledge. What makes me unique is my tenacity and curiosity.
