Who am I?

Phenomenal.

I am a woman in Tech who is highly skilled in Community building. Currently, I am the communities lead at Waape and a Google Women Techmakers Ambassador. As a WTM Ambassador I organise events and positively impact upon women trying to get into tech and grow within in it. I was also a fellow with the SafeSister Fellowship organised by DefendDefenders, a Human Rights Defenders Organisation which enables women to respond to the digital security challenges they face in their work and daily life through a combination of self-study, tool pratice, mentorship, workshops, peer learning and small grants to train their communities. I am also an avid public speaker and in my capacity as one, I have successfully hosted and moderated tech events and panels, most notably Google DevFestKla2020 and 2021, as well as Google I/O Extended Kampala 2022. As a community organiser at Waape, I host bi-weekly Twitter spaces where I host various professionals who share about skills development, job markets and startups. Whilst I've got the time, I blog and TikTok.
